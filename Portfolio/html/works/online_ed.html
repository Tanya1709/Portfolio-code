Overview

Problem

The problem of the current teaching methodology in India is its restrictive nature. The students still follow a particular curriculum where there is hardly any scope for creative thinking and practical knowledge. It lacks discussions, group activities, and debates. There is no element of exploration and experimentation, as the class is mainly focussed on the course contents. The technology of online education is not fully utilized, leading to poor communication and ineffective learning.

Solution



Interactive learning platform that aims to provide a holistic environment to the teacher as well as the students by utilising features like live polling, discussion mode, real-time analysis of student's performance, sound-enabled hand raising, permission required to disable video, a rotating camera keeping an eye on every student, computerized division of groups for group activities, and a record book with class recordings, students participation data. Parents will also be more aware of their ward's performance from a broader perceptive, including active participation, talk time, valuable inputs, leadership qualities, and weaknesses.
