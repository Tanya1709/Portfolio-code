Overview

Introduction

The emergence of online food delivery services such as Zomato and Swiggy has fundamentally changed the way consumers are purchasing food. Despite the eCommerce industry experiencing double digital growth year over year, there are still many products that are not traditionally viewed as items that are sold online. Cakes and bakery goods are one of the non-traditional items.

Objectives

  • Limited options when it comes to creating custom cake.
  • Not an efficient flow to get the perfect cake as wished
  • Creating a more transparent system
  • Providing more customised experience considering different usecases

Result

The goal of the project is to create an e-commerce store for cakes, the concept is to sell cakes online wherein very niche preferences of the user is also taken into consideration to provide with personalised user experience. The online store must allow the consumer to experience the same or similar sensations that they would receive in store with attention to care while creating a personalised experience.
